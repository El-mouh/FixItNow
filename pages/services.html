Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical turnaround time for repairs?

Most computer and smartphone repairs can be completed within 24-48 hours. More complex issues or data recovery services may take longer. Our technicians will provide you with an estimated completion time when you book your service.

Do you offer on-site services or do I need to bring my device to you?

We offer both options! For most services, our technicians can come to your home or office to perform the repair. Alternatively, you can drop off your device at one of our partner locations. You can choose your preferred option during the booking process.

What warranty do you provide on repairs?

We offer varying warranty periods depending on the service. Hardware repairs typically come with a 90-day warranty, while battery replacements have a 12-month warranty. Software services include 30 days of follow-up support. Specific warranty information is provided for each service.

How do I pay for services?

FixItNow offers secure online payment through our platform. You can pay using credit/debit cards or other supported payment methods. For some services, a diagnostic fee may be charged upfront, with the remaining balance due after the repair is completed.

What brands and devices do you support?

Our technicians are experienced with most major brands and devices. For computers, we service all Windows PCs, Macs, and Linux systems. For smartphones, we support iPhone, Samsung, Google Pixel, Huawei, Xiaomi, and many other brands. If you have a specific device, you can check availability during the booking process.
