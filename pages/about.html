About FixItNow

Learn about our mission, team, and commitment to solving tech problems through our trusted marketplace for IT services.

Our Story

FixItNow was founded in 2024 with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between individuals experiencing technical problems and qualified IT technicians who can solve them.

We recognized that many people struggle to find reliable and affordable technical support. Local repair services often lack visibility, and customers may face difficulties in assessing the credibility of technicians. That's why we built FixItNow as a trusted marketplace where users can find, book, and review professional IT services with confidence.

Our platform simplifies access to tech support services by providing an easy-to-use, efficient, and secure platform that connects people with the right expertise for their specific technical issues.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us and guides our decisions every day

Our Mission

At FixItNow, our mission is to revolutionize the way people access IT support by creating a user-friendly, reliable, and efficient platform that connects customers with skilled technicians.

We aim to make technical assistance accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or technical knowledge, while providing new opportunities for IT professionals to grow their businesses through our online marketplace.

Our Values

  • Trust and Reliability: Building confidence through verified technicians and secure transactions.
  • Accessibility: Making tech support available to everyone, everywhere.
  • Quality Service: Maintaining high standards through reviews and ratings.
  • Innovation: Constantly improving our platform for better user experience.
  • Community: Nurturing relationships between technicians and customers.
